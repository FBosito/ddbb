About

The world's first multigenerational Drum & Bass duo.

Drum Dad & Bass Boy unite father and son in a unique DJ and production partnership. This extraordinary dynamic, combined with their distinctive stage presence and a signature sound selection, has quickly established them in the international Drum & Bass scene.

With releases on labels like High Tea, Ridmic (Liquicity) and Fresh Recordings, they rank among the leading Drum & Bass acts in Austria. Their debut EP "Dad's Dubs" entered the iTunes Global Dance Album Charts (peaking at #16) and reached the Beatport Top 100 Drum & Bass Releases.
